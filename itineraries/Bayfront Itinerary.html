gardens by the bay

3 public gardens built on reclaimed land that includes cooled conservatories, individual parks, event and dining spaces, connecting waterways, lakes and aerial bridges. Look for the Cloud Mountain nestled within the Cloud Forest which displays plants from different regions of the world in themed displays. 

directions

From Bayfront MRT Station, leave through Exit B and walk through the underpass. Follow the directions to Gardens by the Bay. Use the camera to capture the dragonfly sculptures, the Supertree Grove opposite the water bank or the red and purple dragonfly bridge on the left for more information. 

admission 

Ticket prices: $ 15 - 28

Operating hours: 0900 - 2100

marina barrage

Singapore's 15th reservoir constructed as part of a national water tap in Singapore. It also acts as a tidal barrier to keep seawater out, helping to alleviate flooding in low lying areas during the rainy season.

directions

From the carpark at Gardens by the Bay, turn left and walk along Marina Gardens Drive to Marina Barrage.

Use the camera to capture the entrance sign or the Pump House logo for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0900 - 2100
