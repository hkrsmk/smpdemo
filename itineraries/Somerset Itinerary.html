orchard central

Singapore's tallest vertical mall featuring 6 super escalators, one which takes shoppers on street level directly up to Level 4. Its Art Trail permanently displays artwork by international artists clustered around the mall. 

directions

From Somerset MRT Station, leave through Exit B and exit 313 @ Somerset.

Turn right and use the camera to capture the red nutmeg structure at the entrance of Orchard Cetral for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 1100 - 2200

hong san see temple

A traditional temple built in tribute to the original Hong San See temple in Nan'an, China, dedicated to the patron deity Guo Sheng Wang. It is Singapore's 1st recipient of the UNESCO Heritage Award.

directions

Cross the road to the Centrepoint and turn right. Walk along Orchard Road and cross  Koek Road. At the bus stop in front of Orchard Plaza, take bus 123 or 143 and alight 2 stops later opposite Shell House.

Turn left and walk along Clemenceau Avenue. Turn right after UE Shopping Mall and walk along Unity Street.

Cross Mohamed Sultan Road and walk between Lam Ann Building and shophouses on the right to Hong San See Temple.

Use the camera to capture the sign at the main entrance of the temple for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0700 - 1800
