haw par villa

A Chinese mythology theme park depicting folk tales, myths and Confucian beliefs. At the 10 Stages of Hell, look for the gruesome punishments portrayed for sinners who have not repented when living, including loansharks being pitchforked by demons in hell.

directions

From Haw Par Villa MRT Station, turn right to Haw Par Villa.

Use the camera to capture the yellow Chinese characters on the artificial rocks at the entrance for more information.
