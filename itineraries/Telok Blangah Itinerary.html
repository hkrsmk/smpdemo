henderson waves

The highest pedestrian bridge in Singapore, this connects Mount Faber Park with Telok Blangah Hill Park, where you can walk through the forest top and watch flora and fauna in their natural habitats. 

directions

From Telok Blangah MRT Station, cross the overhead bridge to Tian Gong Miao Temple. Take bus 176 and alight 2 stops later. Use the camera to capture the Henderson Waves bridge for more information.
