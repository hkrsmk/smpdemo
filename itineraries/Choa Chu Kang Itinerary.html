singapore zoo

Houses the largest captive colony of orangutans in the world. Its exhibits are divided into climatic zones and designed through collaborations with international experts, including Steve Irwin who helped conceptualise the Australian Outback.

directions

From Choa Chu Kang MRT Station, leave through Exit C to the bus interchange. Take bus 927 and alight 18 stops later at the Singapore Zoo.

Use the camera to capture the Singapore Zoo entrance for more information.
