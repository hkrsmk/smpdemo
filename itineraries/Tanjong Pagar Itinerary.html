singapore conference hall

The 1st building to be completed along Shenton Way. It used to hold majority of the large - scale conferences and exhibitions in the late 20th century. Today, it is home to the Singapore Chinese Orchestra and a model of urban architecture in the 1960s.

directions

From Tanjong Pagar MRT Station, leave through Exit E. There should be a Starbucks Coffee to your right. Turn left and walk up some stairs, or a slope. You should see an intersection on your left. Walk along Maxwell Road against the direction of traffic on your side.

At the intersection, turn left and cross the road. Turn right, and cross the road again. You should see Singapore Conference Hall directly ahead.

Use the camera to capture the Singapore Chinese Orchestra logo, the signboard to your far left or the grey address on the far right of the building for more information.
