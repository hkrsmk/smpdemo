eurasian heritage centre

Celebrating the culture of Eurasians who descended from Asian and European expatriates in the colonial era. At Roots of our Community, experience traditional folk dancing and cooking demonstrations while Eurasian Museum features local Eurasian celebrities.

directions

From Dakota MRT Station, leave through Exit A and turn right, walking a bit against the direction of traffic on your side to reach a bus stop. Take bus 16 or 33 for 3 stops. Alight, and walk in the direction of the traffic on your side and turn left into Ceylon Road to walk to Eurasian Heritage Centre.

Use the camera to capture the centre before turning into Ceylon Road, the signboard directing you to the Eurasian Community House, the words "Eurasian Community House" on the wall or the board at the entrance with an introduction of Eurasians in Singapore to learn more.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0900 - 1800

old airport road food centre
Voted as the 1st of the best hawker centres in a recent poll. Its recommendations include satay from Chuan Kee [Stall 85], prawn noodles from Albert Street [Stall 10], chee cheong fun from Freshly Made [Stall 155] and kway chap from Blanco Court [Stall 135].

directions
Walk back out Ceylon Road to the bus stop you came from. Continue walking along Dunman Road, against the direction of traffic on your side. At the intersection between Dunman Road and Haig Road, turn left and cross Dunman Road. Continue walking along Dunman Road in the direction of the traffic on your side, crossing Haig Road. Once you reach a bus stop, take bus 16 or 33 for 3 stops; alight, walk in direction of traffic on your side, cross the first overhead bridge. Continue walking along the road, in the direction of traffic on your side, crossing Jalan Tiga. Walk until you reach Block 51; that is the Old Aiport Road Food Centre.

Continue walking to reach the sign of the hawker centre and capture it with your camera to learn more.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 1000 - 2200
