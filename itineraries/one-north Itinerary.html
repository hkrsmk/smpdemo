fusionopolis

Singapore's first world - class research hub in the computing, media and engineering industries, noted for being an integrated work - live - play - learn complex and its futuristic architectural design as an eco - friendly skyscraper. It houses famed tenants such as A*STAR [Singapore's research board] and the Institute of Infocomm Research where this app was developed.

directions

From one-north MRT Station Exit C, walk up the escalator into the basement of Fusionopolis.

Turn left and walk past the entrance of Connexis Tower out of the building. Use the camera to capture the exterior of Fusionopolis for more information.
