directions

From HarbourFront MRT Station, leave through Exit C. Turn left, walk a bit and then turn right and walk to leave the station. Turn right and walk against the direction of traffic on your side past VivoCity. Cross Sentosa Gateway. Turn right and walk in the direction of traffic on your side down Sentosa Gateway. You should soon see the Power Station to your left.

Use the camera to capture St. James Power Station or its signboard which would be directly above and ahead of you before you turn left into the station for more information.
