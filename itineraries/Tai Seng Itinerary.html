air force museum

Explores the history of aviation in Singapore, based on the theme Men, Machine and Method, through the inclusive use of information technology. Current operations and overseas detachments are also explained through interactive exhibits.

directions

From Tai Seng MRT Station, leave through Exit B and turn left. Walk along Paya Lebar Road against the direction of traffic. At the turn in the road, turn left cross to the other side of the road. Walk straight on along Airport Road past BreadTalk in the direction of traffic on your side. At the next bus stop, take bus 90 and alight 6 stops later. Walk against the direction of traffic on your side until you reach a pedestrian crossing. Turn left and cross the road before walking more against the direction of traffic on your side. You will reach Air Force Museum.

Use the camera to capture the Air Force Museum sign, gate, orange - grey helicopter or orange - white aeroplane for more information.
