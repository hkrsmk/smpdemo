jcube

Home to Singapore's 1st Olympic - size ice rink that has hosted international events and sport competitions such as ice hockey, curling and speed skating. It is designed like an ice cube to reflect sunlight by day and sparkle by night. 

directions

From Jurong East MRT Station, leave through Exit A and walk through the bus interchange. Cross Jurong East Street to JCube.

Use the camera to capture JCube or any entrance labelled JCube for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 1000 - 2200

singapore science centre

Singapore's major science exhibition centre spun off from the National Museum of Singapore as a separate division specialising in the promotion of technological and scientific education. Its Observatory is one of the world's few situated near the Equator.

directions

Cross Jurong East Central 1 and turn left. Walk along the circumference of Jurong Regional Library and cross Jurong Town Hall Road.

Turn right and cross the junction to Singapore Science Centre.

Use the camera to capture the Singapore Science Centre sign for more information.

admission 

Ticket price: $ 8 - 12

Operating hours: 1000 - 1800

omnitheatre

A massive theatre with a hemispheric screen around 5 storeys high, accomodated for upward viewing. The screen stretches from wall to wall, wrapping over the audience such that the images far exceed the field of vision. 

directions

Turn left and walk along Jurong Town Hall Road to the Omnitheatre.

admission 

Ticket prices: $ 7 - 10

Operating hours: 1000 - 1800
