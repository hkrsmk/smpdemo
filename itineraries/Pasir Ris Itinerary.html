wild wild wet

Singapore's largest theme park featuring 9 distinctive rides and facilities. For families, the Shiok River [a river skirting the park perimeter] or the Tsunami: Wave Pool [tidal waves every 15 min] are most suitable for parents to have fun while keeping an eye on their children in the Professor's Playground.

directions

From Pasir Ris MRT Station, take bus 12 and alight 1 stop later at Downtown East. Walk down the stairs and turn right to go into the Downtown East Building. Walk straigh on until you see signs directing you towards Wild Wild Wet!

Use the camera to capture the Wild Wild Wet! logo for more information.
