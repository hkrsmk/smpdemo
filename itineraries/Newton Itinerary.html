newton food centre

A major food centre whose recommendations include the popiah from Bee Heng [Stall 12], carrot cake from Heng Homemade [Stall 28], fishball noodles from Soon Wah [Stall 69] and duck noodles from Kwee Heng [Stall 13]. 

directions

From Newton MRT Station, leave through Exit B and exit into the bus stop. Walk along the road to the overhead bridge beside the roundabout Newton Circus.

Cross the overhead bridge over Clemenceau Avenue to Newton Food Centre.

Use the camera to capture the signage of Newton Food Centre for more information.
