NEWater visitor centre

NEWater is the 3rd of Singapore's 4 national water taps, meeting up to 30% of the country's water needs. It is proof that today's water treatment technologies can treat water of any quality into drinking water. Here, learn about the NEWater purification process and water sustainibility.

directions

From Tanah Merah MRT Station, leave through Exit B and take bus 12, 24, 31 or 38. Alight 2 stops later at Khai Huat Trading.

Turn right and walk along Upper Changi Road East. At the next junction, turn right and cross the road.

Look out for signs directing you towards the NEWater Visitor Centre and walk down Koh Sek Lim Road to the visitor centre.

Alternatively, you can take the shuttle bus at Exit B of Tanah Merah MRT Station during the pickup times of 0845, 1030, 1215, 1400 and 1545 respectively.

Use the camera to capture the NEWater sign at the entrance of the visitor centre for more information.
