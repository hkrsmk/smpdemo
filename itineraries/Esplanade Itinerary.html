raffles hotel

A colonial - style hotel famous for its rich history and Victorian - style architecture. During the Japanese Occupation, it was appointed the living quarters for senior Japanese officers and then a temporary transit camp for prisoners - of - war after Japanese surrender.

war memorial park

Featuring the Civilian War Memorial, its star attraction, this park commemorates the victims of the Sook Ching Massacre, after the Japanese government formally apologised to Singapore for its war crimes.

esplanade theatre

A waterside concert hall and performing arts centre that mainly collaborates with local art groups to foster sustained interest in the arts. Its unique design, shaped in the likeness of the durian, is based on angled glass fins that reflect sun rays at different intervals of the day. 

directions

Cross Bras Basah Road and enter Esplanade MRT Station through Exit F. Leave through Exit D and walk through the underpass.

Use the camera to capture the Esplanade Mall sign for more information.
