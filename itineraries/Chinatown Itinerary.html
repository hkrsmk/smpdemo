chinatown heritage centre

A visual timeline of the local Chinese community telling the story of early immigrants who came here in hopes of a better life. Artefacts displayed include antiques replicating coolie living quarters, shops and clan association houses. 

directions

From Chinatown MRT Station, leave through Exit A and walk straight down Pagoda Street to Chinatown Heritage Centre, a shop house displaying a trishaw at its front.

Use the camera to capture the red sign above, the embossed wooden sign or the trishaw for more information.

admission 

Ticket prices: $ 5 - 8

Operating hours: 0900 - 2000

sri mariamman temple

Singapore's oldest Hindu temple dedicated to Mariamman, the South Indian goddess of rain, revered for her ability to cure diseases. It was founded by Narayana Pillai, a prominent early Indian settler established in the local textile trade.

directions

Cross Pagoda Street to Sri Mariamman Temple. Use the camera to capture the temple entrance for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0700 - 1200 and 1800 - 2100

buddha tooth relic temple museum

A temple built in the Tang dynasty architectural style, housing a relic said to be a tooth from Buddha, found within a mound of solid gold on Bagan Hill in Myanmar. It was gifted to the temple by the Bandula Monastery. 

directions

Turn right and walk along South Bridge Road past shop houses. Cross Temple Street, Smith Street and Sago Street to the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple Museum.

Use the camera to capture the sculpture at the right of the entrance for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0700 - 1900

maxwell food centre

A major food centre whose recommendations include ondeh ondeh from Xing Xing [Stall 31], char kway teow from Marina South Delicious Food [Stall 35], fish soup from King's [Stall 2]  and Cantonese congee from Ho Kee Porridge [Stall 45].

directions

Turn right, and walk along South Bridge Road in the direction of the traffic on your side. Cross the road when you see a pedestrian crossing, and continue in the same direction as before. You will pass Ann Siang Hill and then Erskine Road before reaching Maxwell Food Centre. Use the camera to capture the entrance or the Samsui women sculpture for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0800 - 2200
