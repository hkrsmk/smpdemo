eurasian heritage centre

Celebrating the culture of Eurasians who descended from Asian and European expatriates in the colonial era. At Roots of our Community, experience traditional folk dancing and cooking demonstrations while Eurasian Museum features local Eurasian celebrities.

directions

From Paya Lebar MRT Station, leave through Exit B. Take bus 43 or 76 and alight 4 stops later. Turn left and walk past shophouses.

Turn right into Dunman Road past Tanjong Katong Girls' School and Tanjong Katong Secondary School. Cross Haig Road and walk past Maranatha Seventh - Day Adventist Church.

Turn left and cross Dunman Road into Ceylon Road. Use the camera to capture the entrance of the centre or the metal signboard directing to it for more information.
