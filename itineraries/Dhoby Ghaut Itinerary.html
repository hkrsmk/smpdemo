macdonald house

The site of the infamous MacDonald House bombing, the most serious of 37 bombings by Indonesian saboteurs who sought to disrupt daily life in Singapore as part of its campaign against the formation of Malaysia. This included its disputed territories Sabah and Sarawak. 

directions

From Dhoby Ghaut MRT Station, leave through Exit B and cross the traffic junction at Orchard Road. Turn right and cross Oldham Lane to the MacDonald House.

Use the camera to capture the MacDonald House for more information.

the cathay

A shopping mall that was once Singapore's tallest building which housed Cathay Cinema, the famed Cathay Hotel and Cathay Restaurant. During the World War II and the Japanese Occupation, it functioned as the planning headquarters for military action.

directions

Turn right and walk along Orchard Road. Cross Handy Road and turn left to The Cathay.

Use the camera to capture The Cathay for more information.

Free

istana park

A gateway to the Civic District Trail to educate tourists of Singapore's journey as a nation, designed as an extension to the Istana main entrance. Its centrepiece, the Festival Arch, was inspired by the Istana gates design and rises from a pool of slow - flowing water. 

directions

Turn right and walk back along Orchard Road past MacDonald House. Cross the traffic junction to Exit B, and turn right to the Istana Park.

Use the camera to capture the entrance of the park for more information.

Free
