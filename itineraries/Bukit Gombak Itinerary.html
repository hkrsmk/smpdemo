little guilin

A small park converted from a disused granite quarry. The scenery of its lake containing a large granite rock resembles the natural scenery of Guilin in China, famed for its karst topography where rock surfaces are dissolved by mildly acidic water.

directions

From Bukit Gombak MRT Station, leave through Exit B and walk along Bukit Batok Avenue 5 to Little Guilin.

Use the camera to capture the stone labelled Little Guilin for more information.
