jurong bird park

Asia Pacific's largest bird park, home to more than 8000 birds of 600 different species from around the world. It grew from a small free - flight aviary at Jurong, then an industrial zone with few residential areas, to a globally - acknowledged bird zoo housing 29 threatened bird species.

directions

From Boon Lay MRT Station, leave through Exit D to the bus interchange. Take bus 194 or 251 and alight 1 stop later at Jurong Bird Park.

Use the camera to capture the entrance sign for more information.
