chomp chomp food centre

One of the most famous local food centres, known for its authentic local delicacies. Do try the oyster omelette from Ang Sa Lee Fried Oyster [Stall 26], sambal stingray from Lucy BBQ Seafood [Stall 5 / 6] and Hokkien noodles from Ah Hock [Stall 27].

directions

From Serangoon MRT Station, leave through Exit E. Turn right and walk up sheltered pathway to take bus 315, alighting 5 stops later at Serangoon Garden Circus.

Walk along the roundabout in the direction of the traffic of the roundabout. You should be at the intersection of Kensington Park Road and Farleigh Avenue. Cross Farleigh Avenue and walk along Kensington Park Road in the direction of traffic on your side, and cross the first zebra crossing you see. Continue walking in the direction of traffic on your side, and you will reach Chomp Chomp Food Centre.
