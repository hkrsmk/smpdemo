singapore discovery centre

Singapore's 1st edutainment museum hosting exhibits about its military roots, the vibrant cityscape and the underlying theme that war and peace are 2 sides of the same coin. It also provides both indoor and outdoor venues for hosting special events.

directions

From Joo Koon MRT Station, leave through Exit A. Turn right and walk along Joo Koon Circle and Benoi Road past Union Containers.

Cross the traffic light at Upper Jurong Road and turn left to the Singapore Discovery Centre.

Use the camera to capture the logo sign at the carpark gate for more information.
