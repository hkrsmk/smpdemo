singapore flyer

The world's tallest Ferris wheel in the world with 28 air - conditioned capsules that provide unobstructed panoramic views of the Grand Prix's Marina Bay Street Circuit and the cityscape. At the Retail Terminal, get to experience a Boeing 737NG flight simulator, a 6D motion ride and for the luck - conscious, some good Feng Shui from the Flyer's "double prosperity" blessings.

directions

From Promenade MRT Station, leave through Exit A and cross the Boulevard past Ritz - Cartlon Singapore. Look upwards to your left to see the Singapore Flyer.

Use the camera to capture the Singapore Flyer for more information.

benjamin sheares bridge

Longest and tallest bridge in Singapore named in honour of Singapore's 2nd president. It climbs from street level before soaring over ground and water for full - sized ships to pass under. Its architectural beauty can be clearly seen even from afar.

helix bridge

Pedestrian bridge linking Marina South, East and Centre. Its unique curved structure resembles the DNA helix and is inspired by the yin and yang concept in Asian culture. Also, stand at any of its 5 viewing platforms to watch events taking place at Gardens at the Bay. 

directions

Walk along Temasek Avenue past the Youth Olympic Park to the Helix Bridge.
