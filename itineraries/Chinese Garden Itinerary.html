chinese garden

Designed to be visually exciting and constructed in the Northern Chinese style of the Song dynasty. Climb up the 7 - storey Ru Yun pagoda to get a panoramic view of the garden and the surrounding Jurong landscape. 

directions

From Chinese Garden MRT Station, leave through Exit B and walk along the path to the Chinese Garden.

Use the camera to capture the 7 - storey high pagoda, visible from the MRT station, the Live Turtle and Tortoise Museum sign at the MRT station, or the Chinese Garden direction board for more information.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0600 - 2300

japanese garden

Designed to evoke inner peace and a meditative state, the garden layout references Japan's Muromachi period with its traditional arched bridges, traditional Toro stone lanterns and gravel chipped pavings.

directions

Turn right and walk along the path past the Zodiac Animal Sculptures and between pavilions to the White Rainbow Bridge. Cross the bridge to the Japanese Garden.

admission 

Free

Operating hours: 0600 - 2200
