kranji war memorial

Honouring both local and foreign fighters who died defending Singapore in World War II. The War Memorial houses the 3 military branches: Air Force, Navy, and Army, in memory of more than 24000 names whose bodies were never found.

directions

From Kranji MRT Station, leave through Exit C. Take bus 160, 178, 960, or 961 and alight 3 stops later. Turn left and walk till Woodlands Road.

Turn right and walk along through Woodlands Road through Commonwealth War Graves Commission to Kranji War Memorial.

Use the camera to capture the State Cemetery sign for more information.
