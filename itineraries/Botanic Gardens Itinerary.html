botanic gardens

An extension of the 1st Botanic Gardens at Fort Canning, established by Stamford Raffles, a keen naturalist, wo wanted to evaluate the potential economic importance of cultivation crops, vegetables and spices. 

directions

From Botanic Gardens MRT Station, walk to the Botanic Gardens. Use the camera to capture the entrance gate or the Bukit Timah gate logo for more information.
