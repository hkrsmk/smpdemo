about the app

what

A tourist - based app that provides itineraries and services using image recognition and requires the use of the train system.

who

Tourists and anyone interested in exploring Singapore independently with a trusty tip from time to time.

when/where

Any time, anywhere! As long as you have access to a MRT/LRT train station, that is - and you can travel anywhere in this island!

why

This is a Science Mentorship Programme Project by Li Jia Yang, Ong Zhi Xin Charlotte and Shermaine Si Ying Ying from Raffles Girls' School (Secondary).
